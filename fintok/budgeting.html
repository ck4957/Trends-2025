Recommended Budget Categories

Financial experts typically recommend allocating your income across these major categories:

Housing

25-35% of income

Includes rent/mortgage, utilities, repairs, and home insurance

Transportation

10-15% of income

Includes car payments, gas, maintenance, public transportation

Food

10-15% of income

Includes groceries and dining out

Savings/Debt Repayment

15-20% of income

Includes emergency fund, retirement, and debt payments

Healthcare

5-10% of income

Includes insurance, medications, and medical bills

Personal Spending

5-10% of income

Includes entertainment, clothing, and hobbies

Budgeting Tips for 2025

  • Use AI-powered tools to track spending patterns and identify areas for improvement
  • Prepare for continued inflation by building a larger emergency fund (aim for 6-9 months of expenses)
  • Consider digital banking solutions that offer automated savings and spending insights
  • Review subscriptions regularly - the average person wastes $348 annually on unused subscriptions
  • Implement a "cooling off" period before major purchases (wait 24-48 hours before buying)
  • Allocate funds for upskilling and education to increase earning potential
  • Use cashback apps and credit cards strategically, but avoid carrying balances
